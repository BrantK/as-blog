Allergy Friendly Recipes

Here's some yummy recipes to try that are peanut, tree nut, and egg free. Other ingredients may be substituted to suit your allergy needs as well.

  • Donuts
  • Pancakes

Donuts

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons applesauce
  • 1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Mix together dry ingredients and wet ingredients separately. Mix dry and wet ingredients together. If you don't want to add applesauce, make sure to add more milk or water so the mix doesn't come out too dry!

Pour batter into a greased doughnut pan. Put in oven for 17 minutes. Top with your favorite topping or each plain!

Pancakes

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups water or milk
  • 1/3 cup applesauce

Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. Mix water (or milk) and applesauce together. You can add vinegar in the milk beforehand to make buttermilk pancakes. You can also add a tablespoon of oil here to make the pancakes non-stick if you have a sticky pan!

Mix all the ingredients together, some lumps are okay. Pour into a preheated pan and cook until golden brown on each side.
